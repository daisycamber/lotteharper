Iziphi izinzuzo zokuthatha i-spironolactone njengomuntu wesifazane?


I-Spiro, noma i-spironolactone, ivamise ukunikezwa abesifazane abanokungalingani kwamahomoni. Ezinye zezinzuzo ezingaba khona zokuthatha i-Spiro zihlanganisa:


1. Ukwelashwa kwezinduna: I-Spiro ingasiza ekwehliseni izinduna ngokunciphisa ukukhiqizwa kwe-sebum, engavala izimbotshana futhi iholele ekuqhumeni.


2. Ukunciphisa ukukhula kwezinwele ngokweqile: I-Spiro ingasiza ekunciphiseni ukukhula kwezinwele ngokweqile, okwaziwa nge-hirsutism, ngokuvimbela imiphumela yamahomoni e-androgen.


3. Ukulawulwa kokungalingani kwama-hormone: I-Spiro ingasetshenziswa ukuphatha izimo ezifana ne-polycystic ovary syndrome (PCOS) noma ukungalingani kwama-hormone okungase kubangele ukungahambi kahle kokuya esikhathini noma ezinye izimpawu.


4. Ukwelashwa komfutho wegazi ophakeme: I-Spiro iyisidakamizwa esikhipha amanzi emzimbeni futhi ingasiza ekwehliseni umfutho wegazi ngokunciphisa inani likasawoti namanzi agcinwe emzimbeni.

Ngiyithumela kanjani i-imeyili enohlu lwe-imeyili oluthobelayo kanye nezixhumanisi zokuzikhipha ohlwini?


Ukuthumela i-imeyili enohlu oluthobelayo kanye nesixhumanisi esiqinile sokuzikhipha ohlwini, kufanele landela lezi zinyathelo:


1. Qinisekisa ukuthi uhlu lwakho lwe-imeyili luhambisana nayo yonke imithetho efanele, njengoMthetho we-CAN-SPAM kanye ne-GDPR. Lokhu kusho ukuthi bonke abamukeli kufanele bakhethe ukungena ukuze bathole ama-imeyili akho nokuthi unemvume yabo ecacile yokuxhumana nabo.


2. Faka isixhumanisi esicacile nesivelele sokuzikhipha ohlwini ku-imeyili yakho. Qiniseka ukuthi kulula ukuyithola nokuyichofoza, nokuthi ithatha abamukeli ngokuqondile ibayise ekhasini lapho bengazikhipha khona ohlwini lwakho lwama-imeyili.


3. Ukwenza isixhumanisi sokuzikhipha ohlwini "siqine," ungasebenzisa inketho yokukhipha ukubhalisa ngokuchofoza kanye. Lokhu kusho ukuthi abamukeli bangazikhipha ohlwini lwama-imeyili akho ngokuchofoza kanye nje, ngaphandle kokuthi bagcwalise amafomu engeziwe noma banikeze noma yiluphi ulwazi lomuntu siqu.


4. Uma uthumelai-imeyili, qiniseka ukuthi wenza umlayezo ube ngeyakho futhi ubhekise kumamukeli ngegama uma kungenzeka. Lokhu kungasiza ukukhulisa ukusebenzelana futhi kwenze i-imeyili izizwe ifaneleka kakhulu kumamukeli.


5. Ngaphambi kokuthumela i-imeyili, ihlole ukuze uqinisekise ukuthi isixhumanisi sokuzikhipha ohlwini sisebenza kahle nokuthi i-imeyili ibukeka iyinhle kuwo wonke amadivayisi namaklayenti e-imeyili.


Ngokulandela lezi zinyathelo , ungathumela i-imeyili enohlu oluthobelayo kanye nesixhumanisi esiqinile sokuzikhipha ohlwini ukuze uqinisekise ukuthi abamukeli bakho banolwazi oluhle futhi bangaphuma kalula ekutholeni amanye ama-imeyili avela.

Ngiyibamba kanjani iseva yewebhu ekhaya lami?


Ukusingatha iseva yewebhu ekhaya lakho kungenziwa ngokulandela lezi zinyathelo:


1. Khetha isofthiwe yeseva: Ungakhetha ezinhlelweni zesofthiwe yeseva ehlukahlukene njenge-Apache, i-Nginx, i-Microsoft Internet Information Services (IIS), njll. Khetha evumelana nezimfuneko zakho.



p>2. Setha ikheli le-IP elimile: Xhumana nomhlinzeki wakho wesevisi ye-inthanethi (ISP) futhi ucele ikheli le-IP elimile loxhumano lwakho lwe-inthanethi. Lokhu kuzoqinisekisa ukuthi iwebhusayithi yakho ihlala ifinyeleleka ngisho noma ikheli lakho le-IP lishintsha.


3. Lungiselela umzila wakho: Ngena kuphaneli yokuphatha yomzila wakho bese udlulisela imbobo 80 (imbobo ezenzakalelayo yethrafikhi ye-HTTP) ekhelini le-IP langaphakathi leseva yakho. Lokhu kuzovumela ithrafikhi yangaphandle ukuthi ifinyelele iseva yakho yewebhu.


4. Faka futhi ulungise isofthiwe yeseva: Faka isofthiwe yeseva emshinini wakho weseva futhi uyilungiselele ngokwezidingo zakho,njengokusetha ababungazi ababonakalayo, izitifiketi ze-SSL, njll.


5. Hlola iwebhusayithi yakho: Hlola ukuthi iwebhusayithi yakho iyafinyeleleka yini ngokufaka ikheli lakho le-IP elimile esipheqululini sewebhu. Ungakwazi futhi ukukuhlola usebenzisa amathuluzi aku-inthanethi afana ne-Pingdom noma i-GTmetrix.


6. Igama lesizinda nokusethwa kwe-DNS: Bhalisa igama lesizinda lewebhusayithi yakho bese usetha amarekhodi e-DNS ukuze akhombe ikheli lakho le-IP elingashintshi.


7. Izinyathelo zokuphepha: Sebenzisa izinyathelo zokuphepha ezifana ne-firewall, ukubethela, izipele ezivamile, njll., ukuze uvikele iseva yakho yewebhu ezinsongweni ze-inthanethi.


8. Qaphela futhi unakekele: Njalo qapha iseva yakho ngezinkinga zokusebenza, izinsongo zokuphepha, nezibuyekezo zesofthiwe. Gcina isofthiwe yeseva yakho nezinhlelo zokusebenza zisesikhathini samanje ukuze uqinisekise ukusebenza kahle.

Ngiyakha kanjani iseva yemeyili nge-postfix?


Ukuze wakhe iseva yemeyili nge-Postfix, landela lezi zinyathelo:

< br>

1. Faka i-Postfix: Sebenzisa umphathi wephakheji yakho ukufaka i-Postfix kuseva yakho. Isibonelo, ku-Debian/Ubuntu, ungasebenzisa `sudo apt-get install postfix`.


2. Lungiselela i-Postfix: Amafayela wokumisa we-Postfix atholakala kokuthi `/etc/postfix/`. Ifayela elikhulu lokumisa lithi `main.cf`. Ungahlela leli fayela ukuze umise iseva yakho yemeyili ngokwezidingo zakho. Okunye ukulungiselelwa okuvamile ongase udinge ukukusetha kufaka phakathi igama lesizinda, izilungiselelo zokudlulisa i-imeyili, izizinda ezibonakalayo, njll.


3. Setha amarekhodi e-DNS: Ukuze uqinisekise ukulethwa kwemeyili, udinga ukusetha amarekhodi e-DNS adingekayo (amarekhodi e-MX kanye ne-SPF) esizindeni sakho. Xhumana nombhalisi wesizinda sakho noma umhlinzeki we-DNS ukuze uthole usizo uma kudingeka.

2. Dala uhlaka: Chaza amaphuzu abalulekile esakhiwo, abalingiswa, nezindikimba zencwadi yakho ngaphambi kokuba uqale ukubhala. Lokhu kuzokusiza ukuthi uhlale uhlelekile futhi ugxilile njengoba ubhala.


3. Beka eceleni isikhathi esinikelwe sokubhala: Yakha isimiso sokubhala futhi ubeke eceleni isikhathi esinikelwe usuku ngalunye ukuze usebenze ngencwadi yakho. Lokhu kuzokusiza ukuthi uhlale unomdlandla futhi uthuthuke ekubhaleni kwakho.


4. Vele uqale ukubhala: Kwesinye isikhathi ingxenye enzima kakhulu yokubhala incwadi iyaqala. Ungakhathazeki ngokuphelela kulesi sigaba, vele uqale ukubhala bese ubhala phansi imibono yakho.

3. Dala uhlaka: Hlela imibono yakho namaphuzu ayinhloko ngokwakha uhlaka lokuthunyelwe kwebhulogi yakho. Lokhu kuzokusiza ukuthi uhlele okuqukethwe kwakho ngendlela enengqondo futhi ehambisanayo, okwenza kube lula ngabafundi ukuthi bakulandele.

4. Bhala aisingeniso esiphoqayo: Qala okuthunyelwe kwakho kwebhulogi ngesingeniso esiqinile nesihehayo esidonsa ukunaka komfundi. Sebenzisa ihhuku ukudweba abafundi futhi ubenze bafune ukuqhubeka nokufunda.
